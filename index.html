Career and Technical Education

Learn by doing

Learn the skills you need to start your career! The division of Career & Technical Education at Lewis-Clark State College offers certificates, 2-year, and 4-year degrees to prepare you for high-skill, in-demand careers. We connect students to real careers by making education meaningful through applied learning. Every course is taught by instructors with years of experience in their industry. When you enroll in a BTS program, you will learn by doing and gain the real-world skills that your future employers need.

Gain Real Experience

To graduate, students gain experience through internships, directed studies, and work-based learning opportunities.

Earn as you learn

Many students in internships or work-based learning programs are hired while still enrolled in a program!

Successful Job Placement

Over 95% of graduates who earn their certificate or degree find employment within 6 months after graduation.
